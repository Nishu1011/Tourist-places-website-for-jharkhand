Temples

Jharkhand boasts a vast collection of temples ranging from the prehistoric times to the modern era. The temples in the state offer a glimpse into the rich and diverse cultural heritage of India. Some of the famous temples in the state include the Baidyanath Temple in Deoghar, the Sun Temple in Ranchi, the Jagannath Temple in Ranchi, the Pahari Mandir in Ranchi, and the Angrabadi Temple in Khunti.

Ropeway At Trikut

The three peaks of the TRIKUT hill signify the holy trinity of Brahma , Vishnu and Mahesh. Pilgrims visit this holy site all around the year. DRIL has built a cable car that takes passengers to the top of the hill offering a great view of the holy town of Jasidih, Deogarh.The steep climbing angle and lack of a road to the top of the hill presented a challenge to the construction.

Parasailing training, Giridih

Giridih offers a remarkable space for an exciting aero sport parasailing, in which you will be sailing in the air up to an altitude of 300 feet through a half cut orange shaped parachute called 'Parasail' that is dragged through a rope linked to a jeep on land or by a motorboat above water. You will be having a thrilling and exciting experience at this height in the absence of any engine, sound or pilot.

Popular cities

Ranchi

Ranchi, the capital city of Jharkhand, is the most developed city in the state. The city has several large industries, including the Heavy Engineering Corporation, and is a hub for trade and commerce in eastern India.

Jamshedpur

Jamshedpur, also known as Tata Nagar, is a planned city located in the eastern part of Jharkhand. The city is known for its steel industry and is home to the Tata Steel plant, which is one of the largest steel plants in India.

Dhanbad

Dhanbad is a city located in the western part of Jharkhand and is known for its coal industry. The city has several large coal mines and power plants and is a hub for mining and related industries.

Bokaro

Bokaro is a city located in the eastern part of Jharkhand and is known for its steel industry. The city has several large steel plants, including the Bokaro Steel Plant, which is one of the largest steel plants in India.

Hazaribagh

Hazaribagh is a city located in the central part of Jharkhand and is known for its scenic beauty. The city has several tourist attractions, including the famous Hazaribagh Wildlife Sanctuary.

Deoghar

Deoghar is a city located in the eastern part of Jharkhand and is known for its religious significance. The city has several temples, including the famous Baba Baidyanath Temple, which attracts thousands of visitors every year.

Giridih

Giridih is a city located in the northern part of Jharkhand and is known for its natural beauty. The city is surrounded by hills and forests and is a popular tourist destination.

Ramgarh

Ramgarh is a city located in the central part of Jharkhand and is known for its coal industry. The city has several large coal mines and power plants.

Chaibasa

Chaibasa is a city located in the western part of Jharkhand and is known for its mineral resources. The city has several large mines, including the famous Noamundi Iron Mine.
